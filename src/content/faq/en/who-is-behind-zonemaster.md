---
question: Who is behind Zonemaster?
category: General information
---

Zonemaster is a joint project between [AFNIC](https://www.afnic.fr/en/), the registry of ".fr" TLD and several other TLDs like ".re", ".pm", ".tf", ".wf", ".yt" and ".paris", and [The Swedish Internet Foundation](https://internetstiftelsen.se/en/), the registry of ".se" and ".nu" TLDs.
